How To Play

In Séance, players take on the role of mid-19th century Mediums attempting to communicate with a troublesome Spirit.

This is a co-operative role-playing game. That means you should speak and act as your character would. You’ll also get a chance to play the role of the Spirit.

The ultimate goal is to reveal each Medium’s Message and piece together the story of the Spirit.

What you’ll need:

  1. At least two willing participants.
  2. Writing implements.
  3. Paper.
  4. A 5 minute timer.

To get started:

  1. Read the rules.
  2. Set the mood if you’d like. Candles, crystal balls, and appropriate music can all help players get in to their roles.
  3. Write down your Medium’s name, appearance, 10 Channeling Points, and a Message. Extra details are welcome, but not mechanically significant.
  4. Read the Introduction out loud.
  5. Introduce your Mediums.
  6. Whenever you’re ready to begin in earnest, the player who most recently experienced something unexplainable begins Channeling !e Spirit.
